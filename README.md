Budget Calculator

Take charge of your money. Use this monthly budget calculator to help you manage your spending and understand if you're falling short, breaking even or coming out ahead. Budget Calculator is designed to show total amount of your income and expanses in separated row not just in numbers but in percentage terms. 

Install

The application doesn't require any additional installation. You can start to play by clicking on file “index.html”. Also, you can drag-and-drop it to your browser.
